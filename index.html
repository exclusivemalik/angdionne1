Interface & Usability

In this project, the concentration was on the principle of constituency and standards in User Interface Design. I was tasked with researching websites of my choice and creating my own mock website and mobile mockups based on my research. I love limousines, which is why I geared my research towards limousine websites.
In researching some of my competitors sites, the sites I saw that I consider bad were the ones I came across that I had a hard time finding the information I was looking for quickly such as being able to find where to login to my account and on some sites, I had trouble instantly finding where to reserve a registration online.
The sites I saw that were appealing and made me “want to stay on it” were the sites that I instantly saw a visible button to “book now” or “login” or “request a quote”. After reading whatever the site was saying, it was extremely easy to click on the button I wanted to go to without searching for it.
This project is shows my planning stages (wireframes) to the finished mockup screens.
I had to determine and plan the content requirements for my site as well as the functionality requirements.
  • The content requirements of the site were are a login page
  • Photo gallery of limos
  • Services offered
  • A page to allow booking/reservation thru site
  • Contact page
The colors of the site will be fuchsia, teal, and tan. The fonts may be Roboto and ocassionally Lucida Handwriting. Photos will be of vehicles offered.
